Main Contractor:

ISG

Nature of works:

Demolition

Final value:

2.7 million

Mohan Building Services were awarded the sub contract to carry out the demolition works at the above site, a nine storey building, directly opposite the Central Criminal Court. As well as the court, there was a working pub on the ground floor of the building that remained open throughout, so noise was an issue. Quieter and vibration-free methods had to be used to remove large sections of RC floor slabs, the entire front and rear elevations and two internal staircases. Consequently track and wire sawing and Brokk demolition techniques were employed. MBS also took on board the design (using an external temporary works engineer) and installation of the temporary works systems throughout the building. Also included in the works were significant amounts of non-structural demolition of blockwork walls and other elements and removal of several lifts.
